About the Project

Our 'Messenger' fuctions as an end-to-end web-based communication platform that allows for users to register to communicate with each other through a variety of means. This platform will allow users to send and recieve messages through Public, Private, and Group chats instantaneously. The platform will also allow users to share files and media with each other, as well as create and manage their own profiles.
